Introducing a new gamemode, Blackout Versus! The mod has been updated to Minecraft 1.21.8.

New Gamemode
-
Introducing the new Gamemode: Blackout Versus.
Blackout Versus is exactly like Blackout, except that two teams compete to complete all 25 goals first. The blacklisted goals are the same as those of the Blackout gamemode.
Start a Blackout Versus by creating two teams with players and using `/blackout versus teamA teamB`. Goals completed by both teams show an alternating color highlighting!

- Obtain All Soups

- Get Night Vision From Eating Suspicious Stew

Changes
-
- added Sandstone, Blackstone, and End Stone as options for "Obtain 3 Stone Types"
- added a custom icon for "Reach Sky Limit" goal
- the "Obtain Red Sandstone" goal now requires a Badlands biome near spawn
- goals based on dyed items (dyed concrete, terracotta, stained glass, etc.) can now be rerolled with different colors
- updated Piglin bartering loot tables
- renamed `/blackout start` command to `/blackout solo` for solo Blackout games
- renamed `/blackout team` command to `/blackout coop` for cooperative Blackout games
- player trackers now have Curse of Vanishing
- player names are now colored according to their team color for goal completion messages
- games can no longer be started with 2 of the same players or teams
- Blackout coop can no longer be started with a team containing less than 2 players
- improved goal filters
- removed `/lockout testing`

- fixed "Obtain More..." goals detections and persistence

- fixed biome search for biomes containing warm ocean ruins
