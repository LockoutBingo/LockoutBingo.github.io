Introducing a new gamemode, Blackout! The mod has been updated to Minecraft 1.21.1.

New Gamemode
-
Introducing the new Gamemode: Blackout.
Blackout is similar to Lockout, but everyone plays together to complete all 25 goals.
Because there is no one to compete against, several goals are blacklisted when playing Blackout.
Excluded goals:
- Obtain more blocks/items/XP than opponent goals
- Opponent-related goals
